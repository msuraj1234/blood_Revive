“Donating Money Is Great, But Donating Blood Is Even Better”


1 - Your healing begins with

     charity.

If you’re donating whole blood, we’ll cleanse an area on your arm and insert a brand new sterile needle for the blood draw.A whole blood donation takes about 8-10 minutes.When approximately a pint of whole blood has been collected, the donation is complete and a staff person will place a bandage on arm.


2 - Every drop of blood is like a

     breath for someone!

We’ll sign you in and go over basic eligibility. You’ll read some information about donating blood. We’ll ask you for your complete address. Your address needs to be complete (including PO Box, street/apartment number) and the place where you will receive your mail 8 weeks from donation.


3 - Awareness & Facts

     

The gift of blood helps save lives. Every minute, someone in the world needs blood, and more than 50,000 units of blood are needed each year in India alone. Learn more about blood types and compatibility, different ways to donate and the need for lifesaving blood so you can make an informed decision about donation.

4 - We Value your Suggestions

     

After donating blood, you’ll have a snack and something to drink .You’ll leave after 10-15 minutes and continue your normal routine. Enjoy the feeling of knowing you are helping to save lives. Take a selfie, or simply share your good deed with friends. It may inspire them to become blood donors.
